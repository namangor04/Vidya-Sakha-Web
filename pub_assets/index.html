Vidya-sakha

Towards Digitalization

Our first app towards catering to the educational needs of India & The World...,
Vidya-Sakha, as the name suggests , is a helpful app with simple user interface for
teachers and students. It provides a better platform to connect students and Teachers.

Some Imminent Features

Create Classroom, Digitally

Users can create their own Classrooms and can add students
as well as other Teachers. Classrooms help you to organize the workflow...
Add or Remove Students and/or Teachers whenever you want without alerting them

Give Assignments, Homework

Users can give assignments and homework to the classrooms they have created
or to which they are a part of As a Teacher. Assignments as well as Homework
can contain Images and Documents and can be provided with a deadline to submit...

Video Lectures

Users can send the Video Lecture links to the classrooms they have created or to
the classrooms they are a part of As A Teacher. Users can play YouTube Videos
in Vidya-Sakha itself or choose to play them in YouTube App...

Messages, In Class

Users can message students in the classroom and students can ask doubts
to teachers in the classroom itself. Students/Teachers can attach pictures,
documents as per their needs to make understand better.

Intelligent Features

ESOF™

Encrypted Chats
